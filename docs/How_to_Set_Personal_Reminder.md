**Note:** In most case, you were suggested to set a personal reminder when you create a new job. 

 - **Mobile App**:

  1. At "Sales Connection" Schedule page, click the add button.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_1.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 1" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  2. Select for the job category.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_2.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 2" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  3. Edit the job details.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_3.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 3" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  4. To set personal reminder, click the expand button beside "Reminder".

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_4.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 4" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  5. Click the pencil icon.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_5.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 5" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  6. Click the content box to edit the title of the personal reminder.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_6.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 6" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>
  
  7. Click the expand button beside "Condition" box to select the reminder's condition.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_7.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 7" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>
  
  8. Select the condition. For example, select "before start time", the personal reminder will appears before the job start.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_8.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 8" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

  9. To edit the time type, click the expand button beside "Time Type".

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_9.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 9" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 10. Select for the time type.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_10.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 10" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 11. Click the expand button beside "hour" to change the hour.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_11.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 11" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 12. For example, select 1 hour. It means that the personal reminder will appears before 1 hour of the job.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_12.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 12" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 13. Click the tick button.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_13.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 13" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 14. Make sure the personal reminder's tick button is appear.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_14.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 14" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>

 15. Click the save button to save the job details and set a personal reminder.

     <p align="center">
       <img src="img/App_Personal_Reminder_Step_15.png" alt="App Personal Reminder Step 15" width="280" height="550">
     </p><br>
